I’ve always love staying physically active. Playing volleyball is my absolute favorite. There’s something special about the mix of endurance, focus, and pure zone thinking. Volleyball helped me a lot. I found friends, sense of freedom, and specially to have a strong mind.

Besides playing volleyball, drills is my go-to for building strength. I loved that it’s all about mastering your own body weight. Over time I’ve worked up my contact and my reaction speed increase and I lose a lot of weight — it’s tougher than it sounds!

I also do individual sports, even if they’re not my main thing. Those shared moments build real connections and great memories.

Good nutrition matters too. After a long play or drills. I focus on balanced recovery meals that fuel me right and keep my energy up. Food is fuel, but it’s also part of feeling good overall.
